Dev Build 2.8 (tentative version number)

THIS CHANGELOG IS NOT COMPLETELY COMPREHENSIVE!

1. Enemy waves should be faster, bigger, and more intense

Changes:
1. (BUFF) Gem rewards (from waves) are now slightly more generous and rounded to multiples of 5
2. (MOSTLY NERF) Change enemy coin drop ranges (min, max). Coin drops are triple weighted towards the minimum drop, so changes there are relatively more impactful
	1. Rock Spider (2, 10) -> (1, 4)
	2. Leonard (2, 50) -> (25, 30)
	3. Soap (1, 5) -> (1, 3)
	4. Soap Fragment (1, 5) -> (1, 1)
	5. Flamingo (1, 15) -> (2, 5)
	6. Duck (1, 8) -> (1, 3)
	7. Chicken (1, 10) -> (1, 3)
	8. Crow (1, 15) -> (2, 5)
	9. Ent (1, 18) -> (3, 10)
	10. Gatligator (1, 40) -> (5, 10)
	11. Infector (5, 25) -> (10, 15)
	12. Sentinel (5, 25) -> (4, 10)
	13. Super Sentinel (5, 35) -> (5, 15)
3. (BUFF) Ducks now attack when hurt
4. (BUFF) Flamingo projectiles now home on the nearest player and accelerate faster, but they time out quicker (6 -> 5 seconds)
5. (BUFF) Flamingo Health 10 -> 12
6. (BUFF) Rock Golem health 20 -> 24, speed 0.28 -> 0.32
7. (BUFF) Leonard Health 45 -> 50

8. (NERF) Rainbow Flower Forge cost 5 -> 15
9. (NERF) Crucible now yields 50% less coins (originally the coins were added as a placeholder until gems were fully implemented)
10. (NERF) Mitosis Forge Cost 15 -> 25

Misc:
1. Slightly reduced darkness of shadows 0.95 -> 0.85
2. Adjustments to the shadow shadow to only extend to the opposite direction of the light (to fill in gaps)
	1. This should generally make the shadows more closely follow the shape of the terrain

Fixes:
1. Shadows from Bath Bombs and Cola should now go over walls
2. Shards of Power Forge Cost -3 -> 25
3. Restock sounds will no longer play for far away shops

Changes to the Director System:
1. Chance for Swarm and Circle cards
	1. Base chance 1% -> 5%
	2. Max Chance 25% -> 50%
	3. Rate scaling 1% per wave -> 2.5% per wave
2. Change cost for director to play cards:
	1. Single Spawn c = (1 + 2 * e) -> (1 + e)
	2. Multispawn c = (1 + me) -> (1 + me) (unchanged)
	3. Circlespawn (1 + c + x) -> 0.75cx
	4. The result of these changes is enemies should generally spawn faster and while spawn modifiers are more common, they will be relatively less powerful
3. Base Minimum Credit cost to pass the wave 40 -> 30
	1. This is mostly to counteract the cheaper enemy spawns, though more enemies should still spawn this patch in general
4. Standardized some director card statistics (base times, not including certain modifiers):
	1. Mulligan Time (time before the card can be played after being drawn)
	Single: 4-10 seconds -> 4 + cost / wavemult (wavemult = 1.0 + 0.1 per wave) seconds
	Multi: 10 seconds -> same as Single
	2. Play Recoil (time before another card can be played after this card has been played)
	Single: 1-2 seconds -> 0.5 + 0.25 * cost seconds
	Multi: 5 seconds -> same as Single
	Wavemult also applied here, but is not part of the base algorithm
	3. Boths stats receive a 80% to 120% random multiplier after being selected
5. The Mulligan Time is now speed up by the wave power stat, in addition to ambush stat
6. The first cards drawn at the start of a wave now bypass 70% of their mulligan time, leading to faster wave starts
7. Swarm Circle spawns may now have 2 portals instead of minimum 3 (less of a circle shape and more of a line)
8. Multispawns no longer have 2-3 enemies to start with (only 2, then rolls for extra)
9. Director credit gathering speed 1.1 -> 1.0
10. Random 1/1000 per tick card mulligans no longer happen. If director is stuck with a ton of expensive cards, they can no longer be randomly replaced.
11. Director now only holds 6 -> 5 cards at once, increasing the chance they will get a hand of expensive cards
12. Wave power growth rate (rate at which it permanently grows every 3rd wave) increased 240 + 20 * scaling factor -> 240 + 30 * scaling factor
13. Enemy Health growth rate 10% + 5% * scaling factor -> 5% + 5% * scaling factor^2
	1. Removed the large 100% permanent health scaling from waves 20, 30, 40 (the square scaling will outpace it)
14. Permanent bonus skull wave scaling moved from wave 17 -> wave 14
	1. Removed the temporary skull wave modifier on wave 15, 20, 25, etc.
15. On wave 4, enemies permanently gain 10% max health
16. Director will now start with waveNum bonus credits on wave start, allowing more immediate purchasing of enemy spawns
